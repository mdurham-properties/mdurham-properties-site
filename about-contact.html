About M.Durham Properties

M.Durham Properties and Rentals was founded with a mission: to make housing more accessible and trustworthy for everyone. Inspired by personal challenges faced during childhood, Milo Durham created this platform to help families find stable, affordable housing with ease.

Meet the Founder

Milo Durham turned a difficult housing experience into a passion for real estate. Today, M.Durham Properties offers listings for rent and sale, ensuring others don’t go through what his family did. Milo’s goal is simple: a home for everyone.
