Why Choose Us

We believe that real estate isn’t about transactions — it’s about people. Our mission is to create a seamless experience that leaves you confident, comfortable, and excited for what’s next.

Meet the Founder: Milo Durham

“Growing up, I saw firsthand how hard it can be when families struggle with housing. My parents faced serious challenges when I was a child — finding a stable, safe place to live wasn’t always easy. That experience never left me. It became the reason I chose this path.”

M.Durham Properties and Rentals was born from that motivation: to help others find the security, peace, and pride of a place to truly call home.
