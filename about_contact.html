About M.Durham

Milo Durham started this business out of a personal passion for helping people find secure, affordable housing — a value rooted in childhood experiences. Today, M.Durham Properties and Rentals is committed to offering reliable, personalized services for both property seekers and owners.

Meet the Founder

Hi, I’m Milo Durham. My journey into real estate started from something deeply personal: growing up, my parents faced real struggles with housing — and that stuck with me. That experience became my motivation. Today, I’ve turned that pain into purpose, helping others find homes that feel secure, affordable, and right.
