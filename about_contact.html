M.Durham Properties and Rentals was founded by Milo Durham with a passionate vision: to help others find stable, welcoming homes. Growing up, Milo witnessed his own family struggle with housing, an experience that planted the seed for what is now a thriving property sales and rental business.

We specialize in making the property process as smooth, personal, and professional as possible — whether you're looking to buy, rent, or list a home.

Meet the Founder

Hi, I'm Milo Durham. My journey started from a deep place — seeing my parents go through tough times due to housing challenges. I promised myself I’d build something that could make it easier for others to find security and peace through quality homes. That’s what drives me every single day.
