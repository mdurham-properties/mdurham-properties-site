About M.Durham Properties and Rentals

At M.Durham Properties and Rentals, we understand that real estate is more than just buying or renting — it’s about security, comfort, and finding a place to truly call home.

Our company was founded with one simple mission: to help individuals and families find safe, affordable housing solutions in a market that often overlooks them. With deep knowledge of the local community and a heart-driven approach, we’re committed to delivering value through transparency, empathy, and a personalized experience every step of the way.

Meet the Founder

Hi, I’m Milo Durham. My journey into real estate wasn’t born out of opportunity — it was born out of hardship.

Growing up, my parents struggled with housing, and I saw firsthand how unstable living conditions can affect a family. That personal experience became my motivation. I knew then that if I ever had the chance, I’d do something to change that for others.

Today, M.Durham Properties and Rentals is the result of that mission. I’m proud to help others find homes where they can finally feel settled, safe, and seen.
