About M.Durham Properties and Rentals

M.Durham Properties and Rentals is more than just a real estate business — it's a mission driven by personal experience. Founded by Milo Durham, the company was born out of a deep-rooted passion to help others find safe, affordable, and secure housing. Having experienced the hardship of unstable housing during childhood, Milo turned that struggle into a lifelong commitment to assist others in finding the homes they deserve. With local expertise and a client-first approach, we aim to make renting and owning property a stress-free journey.

Meet the Founder

Milo Durham is the heart behind M.Durham Properties and Rentals. With a story fueled by determination and empathy, Milo transformed adversity into action. His vision is to build a real estate service that cares — not just about transactions, but about people. His journey continues to inspire the way we do business every day.
