About M.Durham Properties and Rentals

M.Durham Properties and Rentals was founded with a vision to provide quality housing solutions. Our mission is to help people find their ideal homes with ease and confidence.

Driven by personal experience, founder Milo Durham turned childhood housing challenges into motivation to create a company that puts people first.

Meet the Founder

Name: Milo Durham

Motivation: Growing up, Milo experienced the struggles of housing instability firsthand. This life experience inspired a deep commitment to help others find safe, affordable, and beautiful homes.
